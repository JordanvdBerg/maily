Welcome to Trailer Land Park

Our tranquil trailer park offers spacious, shaded pitches for your own RV or trailer. Nestled amidst rolling hills and lush greenery, it’s the perfect base for exploring the local region or simply unwinding by the campfire.

We provide essential amenities including clean ablution blocks, fresh water hook-ups, electricity points, and a communal braai area. Experience the peace and quiet, connect with nature, and make lasting memories.

Meet Hardus, The Heart of Trailer Land

Hardus van der Berg, Founder

Hello, I'm Hardus van der Berg, and Trailer Land is more than just a business to me—it's a lifelong passion project born from countless road trips and a deep love for the outdoors. After years of exploring hidden gems and camping under starlit skies, I decided to create a place where others could experience the same freedom and joy.

My vision for Trailer Land was simple: to offer a welcoming retreat for travelers, whether they bring their own home on wheels or choose one of our meticulously maintained motorhomes. Every detail, from the peaceful park setting to the comfort of our vans, is designed to ensure you have an exceptional and memorable journey.

I believe in the power of travel to connect us with nature and with each other. Come and experience the warmth and adventure of Trailer Land. I look forward to welcoming you!
